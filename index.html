Beyond Morality
Fate's Tempting Odyssey

Overview

Dive into Beyond Morality, a thrilling interactive story that challenges the rules of right and wrong. In this visual novel, you play a 21-year-old facing tough times in a world overrun by organized crime. Your choices decide whether things get better or worse.

Navigate the twists of fate, meet diverse characters with their own stories, and decide how you'll handle the pressure. Will you stay in control, be the best you can be, or give in to the temptations of lust and power?

Explore Beyond Morality, where your decisions shape the story, and morality isn't black and white. Choose wisely as you unravel a world where every choice leads to a new possibility. Will you follow the path of virtue, or will you carve your own destiny in the enticing realms of temptation? The journey is yours to decide in Beyond Morality.

Key Features

  • Interactive Storytelling
  • Dystopian Realism
  • Morality Dilemmas
  • Multidimensional Characters
